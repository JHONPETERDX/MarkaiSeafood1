Who are we?

DEXIM is an exporting company that, since 1991, has carried out an uninterrupted business activity in the areas of fishing and processing of a wide variety of high quality frozen hydrobiological products.

DEXIM has a large plant a few meters from the unloading dock that meets all the design requirements that guarantees an adequate process. Its modern furniture, precision equipment and water treatment are managed by highly qualified personnel who, since 1996, apply the HACCP Plan where each step of the production cycle is monitored to guarantee the highest quality and freshness that DEXIM offers.

DEXIM's export value exceeds $ 20 million dollars annually and it is a source of employment for more than 700 workers. All the executive staff of the company is from Paita and works in the company since the beginning, with which it has grown and continues to grow economically and professionally together with DEXIM.

DEXIM is an active member of the Committee for Fisheries and Aquaculture of the National Society of Industry of Peru.

Where are we?

DEXIM is located in Paita, in the main fishing port for human consumption in Peru.
